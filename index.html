About Averon

Averon (AVN) is a decentralized utility token deployed on the Polygon network. The project focuses on transparency, simplicity, and long-term sustainability.

AVN follows a fixed supply model with no mint function and avoids short-term hype. Growth is driven organically through open market participation.
